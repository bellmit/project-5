FBA US: Inventory Requirements

If import to UK

Check plug has either (i) a UK 3-pin plug, or (ii) a non-UK plug that has been permanently fitted with a UK safety approved conversion plug which cannot be removed without use of a tool. (Note that it is not lawful to just supply a loose adapter (such as a travel adapter) which can be removed by hand.)

Checklist - Sample evaluation (PS) *
Receive sample
Update package info to DRS.network. Check product info on Brand website
Check complexity of product (possible compatibility issues that can lead to lots of customer care communication. Remind supplier of regional differences (ex. K526 Pawbo local network, K5-2C oral irrigator)
Check overall product quality (appearance, quality and crucial function)
Check packaging (appropriate language, compliance labels, barcode space)
Check user manual, online manual, system, APP
Check for warranty content. Share articles with supplier [Most EU countries provide for a minimum period of 2 years from the date the customer received the product]
Share packing instruction with supplier (產品及外箱包裝說明, 加拿大包裝語言)
Identify problems with supplier
Confirm whether to provide TTS care card (not suitable for commingled products)
